Overview of Eligibility

The Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) is governed by specific eligibility norms set by the Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG). Before filling out the application form, candidates must ensure they meet the **REAP 2026 Eligibility Criteria** regarding academic qualification, age, and domicile.

Admission to B.E./B.Tech and B.Arch courses in Rajasthan is strictly merit-based. While there is no separate state-level entrance exam, the eligibility criteria act as the primary filter for candidates. Whether you are applying through JEE Main rank, Class 12th percentile, or Diploma marks, meeting these standards is mandatory.

Candidates belonging to reserved categories should also check the Reservation Policy for specific relaxations in marks and age.

Educational Qualification for B.E. / B.Tech

1. For Class 12th Passed Candidates

Candidates must have passed the 10+2 examination from a recognized board (RBSE/CBSE/ICSE) with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of the following subjects: Chemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, Technical Vocational subject, Computer Science, Information Technology, Informatics Practices, Agriculture, Engineering Graphics, or Business Studies.

  • General Category: Must have obtained at least 45% marks in the above subjects taken together.
  • Reserved Category: Candidates belonging to SC/ST/Non-Creamy Layer OBC/Non-Creamy Layer MBC/PwD of Rajasthan State must have obtained at least 40% marks.

2. For Diploma Holders

Candidates who have passed a minimum 3-year Diploma examination with at least 45% marks (40% for reserved category candidates of Rajasthan) are also eligible for admission to the first year of B.E./B.Tech courses.

Note: Diploma holders can also apply for Lateral Entry (2nd Year) via LEEP, but REAP is primarily for 1st-year admission.

Educational Qualification for B.Arch

Admission to Architecture courses is governed by the Council of Architecture (COA). The REAP 2026 Eligibility Criteria for B.Arch are stricter:

  • Passed 10+2 scheme of examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics subjects.
  • Passed 10+3 Diploma Examination with Mathematics as a compulsory subject.
  • NATA Score: The candidate needs to qualify for an aptitude test in architecture conducted either by NTA (i.e., JEE) or NATA conducted by the Council of Architecture.
Age Limit and Domicile Criteria

Age Limit

Candidates whose date of birth falls on or after October 1, 2001 are eligible. (Note: This date is tentative based on previous years' trends for REAP 2026).

  • Relaxation: In the case of Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), and Person with Disability (PwD) candidates, the upper age limit is relaxed by 5 years (Born on or after October 1, 1996).
  • Date of birth as recorded in the Secondary Education mark sheet/certificate only will be taken as authentic.

Domicile Requirements

REAP divides candidates into two main categories for seat allocation:

  1. Rajasthan State (RS) Candidates: Candidates who have a valid domicile of Rajasthan. They are eligible for the majority of seats (approx 85%) and all state-specific reservations.
  2. Outside Rajasthan (OR) Candidates: Candidates from other states. They are eligible for 15% of the seats under the General category. They cannot claim reservation benefits (SC/ST/OBC) of their home state in Rajasthan.
Medical Fitness

A candidate must be medically fit to undertake the course. A Medical Fitness Certificate signed by a Medical Officer of the Government of Rajasthan or a Registered Medical Practitioner is required at the time of reporting.

The candidate should not suffer from any disease or mental infirmity that would interfere with their studies or the duties of a professional engineer.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: What is the minimum percentage required for REAP 2026?

General category candidates need at least 45% marks in Class 12th (Science). For SC/ST/OBC/MBC/PwD candidates of Rajasthan, the minimum requirement is 40%.

Q2: Is JEE Main 2026 mandatory for REAP?

No, JEE Main is not mandatory. However, REAP gives first priority to JEE Main rank holders. Seats are then filled based on Class 12th percentile and Diploma marks.

Q3: Can Diploma holders apply for REAP 2026?

Yes, candidates with a 3-year Diploma in Engineering/Technology are eligible for first-year B.Tech admission via REAP.

Q4: What is the age limit for REAP 2026?

Generally, candidates should be born on or after October 1, 2001. There is a 5-year relaxation for SC/ST/PwD candidates. (Dates are tentative based on previous years).

Q5: Can students from outside Rajasthan apply?

Yes, 15% of seats are reserved for Outside Rajasthan (OR) candidates. They are considered under the General category.
