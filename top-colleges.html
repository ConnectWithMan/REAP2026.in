How to Choose a College

Selecting the right institute is pivotal for your career. Beyond rankings, consider these critical parameters before locking your choices in REAP:

1. Placements & Internships

  • Consistency: Check the placement records for the last 3-5 years, not just the current year.
  • Average vs. Highest Package: Don't get swayed by a single high package. The average package reflects the reality for most students.
  • Recruiters: Look for core companies if you are in branches like Mechanical or Electrical, and top tech firms for CS/IT.

2. Faculty & Infrastructure

  • Qualified Faculty: Ensure the department has experienced professors, preferably with Ph.D. degrees.
  • Labs & Research: Engineering is practical. Check if the college has well-equipped labs and innovation centers.
  • Location: Colleges in hubs like Jaipur or NCR often have better industry connect and exposure.
