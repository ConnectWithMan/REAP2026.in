Documents Required for REAP 2026 Counselling

Complete Checklist for Verification & Admission

Why Documentation Matters

Participating in the Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) is the first step toward a successful engineering career in the state. However, ensuring you have the correct Documents Required for REAP 2026 Counselling is the most critical step in securing your seat. Every year, numerous candidates face difficulties or even rejection during the document verification phase simply because they lack a specific certificate or their documents are not in the prescribed format.

The Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG), Rajasthan, maintains strict protocols regarding documentation to ensure transparency and eligibility. Whether you are applying through JEE Main scores, Class 12th percentile, or Diploma marks, the physical verification of documents is mandatory at the reporting institute. This comprehensive guide covers every single document you will need, from application to final reporting.

Before proceeding, make sure you have checked the Reservation Criteria to understand which category-specific documents apply to you.

Quick Checklist: Documents Required for REAP 2026

Below is the summary list of documents you must arrange in original and multiple sets of self-attested photocopies. We will discuss each in detail in the subsequent sections.

  • Aadhar Card / Valid Photo ID Proof
  • Class 10th Marksheet & Certificate (Proof of Date of Birth)
  • Class 12th (10+2) Marksheet & Certificate
  • JEE Main 2026 Score Card (If applying through JEE quota)
  • Diploma Marksheet (For Diploma holders)
  • Domicile Certificate (For Rajasthan State Candidates)
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS - if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (For TFWS seats or EWS category)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
  • Undertaking/Self-Declaration (As per REAP format)
  • Passport Size Photographs (Recent, color)
  • Provisional Seat Allotment Letter (Generated after counselling)
Detailed Breakdown of Essential Certificates

1. Educational Qualification Documents

Your academic records are the primary basis for your merit calculation (especially if you are applying via Class 12th percentile). Ensure that:

  • 10th Class Marksheet: This serves as the primary proof for your Date of Birth and Father's/Mother's name spelling. The details on your REAP application form must match this document exactly.
  • 12th Class Marksheet: You must produce the original marksheet showing you have passed with the required percentage (45% for General, 40% for Reserved categories). If your result is awaited, you may need a provisional certificate from your school.
  • JEE Main 2026 Score Card: Since REAP gives first priority to JEE Main rank holders, this document is vital if you have taken the exam. Ensure you download the final rank card from the NTA website.

2. Identity and Nativity Proofs

To claim the "Rajasthan State Candidates" quota, which constitutes the majority of seats, you must prove your domicile status.

  • Domicile Certificate: Issued by the competent authority (Tehsildar/Magistrate) of Rajasthan. This proves you are a bona fide resident of the state.
  • Aadhar Card: Mandatory for identity verification. Ensure your biometric details are updated.

3. Category and Reservation Documents

If you are applying under any reserved category, the specific Documents Required for REAP 2026 Counselling include:

  • Caste Certificate (SC/ST): Issued by the competent authority.
  • OBC/MBC Certificate (Non-Creamy Layer): This certificate must be valid and issued on or after 1st April 2025 (or as per specific REAP 2026 guidelines). Older certificates may require an affidavit.
  • EWS Certificate: For Economically Weaker Sections. It must be valid for the financial year 2026-27.
  • PWD Certificate: For Persons with Disability, issued by a medical board with 40% or more disability.
  • KM (Kashmiri Migrant) / PM (Prime Minister Special Scholarship Scheme) certificates if applicable.

For more details on seat distribution, visit our Top Colleges page to see seat matrices.

4. Income Certificate

An Income Certificate is mandatory if:

  • You are applying for the Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme (TFWS).
  • You are claiming OBC-NCL or EWS status (as supporting proof of income).

The certificate should be issued by a Tehsildar or equivalent authority and must reflect the annual family income from all sources.

5. Medical Fitness Certificate

A Medical Fitness Certificate is a mandatory document for all candidates. It certifies that you are medically fit to pursue technical education. The format is usually provided in the REAP information brochure and must be signed by a Medical Officer (Government Doctor).

Documents Required for Reporting at Institute

Once a seat is allotted to you in the counselling rounds, you must physically report to the allotted college. At this stage, the Documents Required for REAP 2026 Counselling verification process is final. In addition to the certificates mentioned above, you will need:

Document Name Purpose
Provisional Seat Allotment Letter Proof that a seat has been allocated to you by the REAP authority. Download this from the candidate login.
Application Form Printout Copy of the filled REAP 2026 application form with your photo and signature.
Fee Receipt Proof of payment of the advance admission fee or registration fee.
Transfer Certificate (TC) Original TC from the last attended institution (School/College).
Migration Certificate Required if you are coming from a board other than the Rajasthan Board (RBSE), though often asked from all students.
Character Certificate Issued by the Principal of the last attended school/college.
Warning: Failure to produce original documents at the time of reporting will lead to the cancellation of your admission.
Common Mistakes to Avoid with Documents

Even with the correct documents, students often face issues due to minor oversights. Here are common mistakes regarding the Documents Required for REAP 2026 Counselling:

  • Name Mismatch: Ensure your name on the Aadhar card matches exactly with your 10th and 12th marksheets. If there is a discrepancy, get an affidavit prepared immediately.
  • Expired Certificates: Category certificates, especially OBC-NCL and EWS, have a validity period (usually one financial year). Ensure yours is current for the 2026-27 session.
  • Laminated Originals: Some colleges refuse to accept laminated original marksheets as they cannot verify the authenticity of the paper or stamps. Avoid laminating documents before verification.
  • Missing Signatures: Ensure that self-attested copies are actually signed by you. Unsigned copies may be rejected.
  • Blurry Scans: When uploading documents during the online registration, ensure the scans are high-resolution and legible. Blurry documents can lead to the rejection of your application form.
Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Is the Domicile Certificate mandatory for all candidates?

No, the Domicile Certificate is mandatory only for candidates claiming seats under the Rajasthan State Quota. Candidates from Outside Rajasthan (OR) do not need a Rajasthan domicile certificate but must produce their own state's relevant ID proofs.

Q2: Can I submit my documents online?

During the registration phase, you are required to upload scanned copies of the Documents Required for REAP 2026 Counselling. However, physical verification of original documents happens at the allotted institute during reporting.

Q3: What if my Class 12th result is awaited?

You can apply as an "Appearing" candidate. However, at the time of reporting, you must produce the original marksheet or a provisional certificate from your board/school proving you have passed with the required percentage.

Q4: How old can the OBC-NCL certificate be?

Usually, the OBC-NCL certificate should be issued within the last 6 months to 1 year from the date of counselling. If it is older, you may need to submit an affidavit stating you still fall under the Non-Creamy Layer. Check the specific REAP 2026 brochure for the exact date cutoff.

Q5: Where can I find the Medical Fitness Certificate format?

The format is typically available in the official REAP Information Brochure. You can download it from the official website, print it, and get it signed by a registered Government Medical Officer.
