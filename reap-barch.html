REAP B.Arch 2026

Rajasthan Architecture Admission Process

For B.Arch Admissions

Overview

The Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG) in Rajasthan organizes the REAP (Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process), the gateway for ambitious students to pursue B.Arch (Architecture) courses in Rajasthan. This state-level process, supported by government-aided and private unaided technical institutions, offers a fair and competitive selection based on NATA scores or JEE Main Paper 2 merit. Recognized diploma holders are also eligible. REAP B.Arch 2026 features three rounds of counseling, open to candidates from Rajasthan and other states.

Eligibility Criteria for REAP B.Arch 2026
  • Applicants must have completed or appeared in 12th grade with Mathematics as a compulsory subject.
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, and 50% marks in aggregate of the 10+2 level examination.
  • Or, candidates who have passed a 10+3 Diploma Examination with Mathematics as a compulsory subject with at least 50% marks in aggregate.
  • A valid NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture) score or JEE Main Paper 2 score is mandatory for admission.
How to Apply for REAP 2026

Candidates willing to apply for REAP 2026 must follow the steps below to complete the online registration process:

  • Step 1: Online Registration: Visit the official REAP website and register using a valid email ID and mobile number.
  • Step 2: Fee Payment: Pay the non-refundable application fee of Rs. 885/- via Net Banking, Credit Card, or Debit Card.
  • Step 3: Fill Application Form: Log in with your credentials and fill in personal, academic, and contact details accurately.
  • Step 4: Upload Documents: Upload scanned copies of your photograph, signature, and relevant mark sheets as per the prescribed format.
  • Step 5: Choice Filling: Select your preferred colleges and branches. It is advisable to fill in maximum choices to increase the chances of seat allotment.
  • Step 6: Lock Choices: Lock your choices before the deadline. If not locked, the system will automatically lock the saved choices.
Documents Required

Candidates must produce the following original documents along with self-attested copies at the time of reporting to the allotted institute:

  • Aadhaar Card or valid Photo ID proof.
  • 10th Class Marksheet and Certificate (Date of Birth Proof).
  • 12th Class Marksheet and Certificate.
  • NATA 2026 Score Card or JEE Main Paper 2 Score Card.
  • Domicile Certificate (for Rajasthan candidates).
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS) issued by competent authority.
  • Income Certificate (for TFWS candidates).
  • Medical Fitness Certificate.
