RMAP 2026

Rajasthan MBA Admission Process

Centralized MBA Counselling for Rajasthan

Overview

RMAP 2026 (Rajasthan Management Admission Process) is the centralized admission process conducted by the Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG), Jaipur on behalf of the Department of Technical Education, Rajasthan for admission to MBA programs in participating colleges across Rajasthan. The process provides a fair and transparent selection mechanism based on valid entrance exam scores including CAT, XAT, CMAT, ATMA, MAT, and GMAT. RMAP 2026 includes Government MBA Colleges, Government-Aided Institutions, Private MBA Colleges, and Self-Financed Management Institutions across Rajasthan, offering opportunities to candidates from Rajasthan and other states.

Eligibility Criteria for RMAP 2026
  • Hold a Bachelor's Degree of minimum 3 years duration.
  • Obtain at least 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved category candidates).
  • Final year students may apply subject to eligibility fulfillment.
  • Valid entrance exam score (CAT, XAT, CMAT, ATMA, MAT, or GMAT) is preferred for merit-based selection.
  • Rajasthan domicile is preferred but not mandatory for participation.
How to Apply for RMAP 2026

Candidates willing to apply for RMAP 2026 must follow the steps below to complete the online registration process:

  • Step 1: Registration: Register online and create account on the official RMAP portal.
  • Step 2: Fee Payment: Pay the registration fee of ₹885 (₹750 + ₹135 GST) via online payment gateway.
  • Step 3: Form Filling: Enter personal, academic, and category details accurately.
  • Step 4: Document Upload: Upload scanned copies of all required documents as per prescribed format.
  • Step 5: Merit Preparation: Merit list will be prepared according to priority rules based on entrance exam scores and domicile status.
  • Step 6: Choice Filling: Select your preferred MBA colleges and specializations. Fill maximum choices to increase seat allotment chances.
  • Step 7: Choice Locking: Lock your choices before the deadline.
  • Step 8: Seat Allotment: Seats will be allotted based on merit and preferences.
  • Step 9: Reporting: Report to the allotted institute and pay seat acceptance fee for admission confirmation.
Documents Required

Candidates must produce the following original documents along with self-attested copies at the time of reporting to the allotted institute:

  • Graduation Marksheet
  • Degree/Provisional Certificate
  • Class 10 Marksheet
  • Class 12 Marksheet
  • CAT/XAT/CMAT/MAT/ATMA/GMAT Score Card (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS/PwD)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Signature

Priority 1 Category-A Sports Quota (as per Rajasthan Government policy)
Priority 2 Valid CAT/XAT/CMAT/ATMA/MAT/GMAT scores from Rajasthan
Priority 3 Valid CAT/XAT/CMAT/ATMA/MAT/GMAT scores from outside Rajasthan
Priority 4 Candidates with Rajasthan domicile
Priority 5 Candidates without Rajasthan domicile
Application Fee
  • Registration Fee: ₹885 (₹750 Registration Fee + ₹135 GST)
  • Seat Acceptance Fee: ₹10,000 (adjusted against institute fees)
Reservation Policy

Reservation is provided as per Rajasthan Government rules for:

  • SC (Scheduled Caste)
  • ST (Scheduled Tribe)
  • OBC (Other Backward Classes)
  • MBC (Most Backward Classes)
  • EWS (Economically Weaker Section)
  • PwD (Persons with Disability)
  • Defence
  • Sports Quota
