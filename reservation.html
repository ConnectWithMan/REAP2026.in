REAP 2026 Reservation Policy

Know Your Rights & Seat Quotas

SC, ST, OBC, EWS, MBC, PwD & TSP Rules Explained.

Seat Matrix & Quotas
Vertical Reservation

As per the Government of Rajasthan norms, seats are reserved for candidates belonging to Rajasthan Domicile in the following categories. Candidates must produce a valid category certificate during reporting.

Category Full Form Reservation %
SC Scheduled Caste 16%
ST Scheduled Tribe 12%
OBC-NCL Other Backward Class (Non-Creamy Layer) 21%
MBC-NCL More Backward Class (Non-Creamy Layer) 5%
EWS Economically Weaker Section 10%
Horizontal Reservation

Horizontal reservation cuts across the vertical reservation categories. This means these seats are adjusted within the respective category (General, SC, ST, etc.) of the candidate.

1. Women Candidates

  • 25% Reservation: 25% of seats in each category (General, SC, ST, OBC, etc.) are reserved for women candidates.
  • Conversion: If women candidates are not available, these seats are converted to male candidates of the same category.

2. Persons with Disability (PwD)

  • 5% Reservation: Reserved for candidates with at least 40% disability.
  • Certificate: A valid disability certificate from a competent medical authority is mandatory.

3. Defence Personnel (Ex-S)

  • Priority is given to wards of defence personnel killed in action, disabled in action, or ex-servicemen.
  • Requires a certificate from the Zila Sainik Welfare Board.
Special Quotas & TSP

Apart from standard reservations, REAP includes provisions for specific regions and schemes.

Tribal Sub Plan (TSP)

  • 45% ST Reservation: In colleges located in TSP areas (like Banswara, Dungarpur, Pratapgarh), 45% of seats are reserved for ST candidates of the TSP area.
  • Domicile: Candidates must produce a special bona-fide resident certificate of the TSP area.

Supernumerary Seats

  • TFWS (Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme): 5% extra seats in private colleges for candidates with family income less than ₹8 Lakhs/annum. Tuition fee is waived.
  • Kashmiri Migrants (KM): 5% seats reserved for migrants from J&K.
  • PMSS: Scholarship scheme for J&K and Ladakh students (Admissions usually via AICTE portal).
