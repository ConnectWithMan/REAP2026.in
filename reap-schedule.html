REAP 2026 Counselling Schedule

Official Counselling Schedule and Guidelines for B.E. / B.Tech. Admissions in Rajasthan

About REAP 2026

The Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) is the centralized counselling process organized for admissions to undergraduate engineering programs (B.E. / B.Tech) across various government and private technical institutes in Rajasthan.

Admissions are primarily based on JEE Main scores, followed by Class 12th merit for vacant seats. Priority is given to candidates with Rajasthan domicile, though candidates from other states are also eligible under specific quotas.

Important Instructions

  • Candidates must register before the specified deadlines.
  • Ensure all uploaded documents are clear, valid, and recent.
