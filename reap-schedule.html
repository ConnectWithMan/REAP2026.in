REAP 2026 Important Dates

The Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) 2026 schedule outlines all the critical deadlines for B.Tech/B.E. admissions in Rajasthan. Candidates must closely follow these dates to ensure they do not miss out on registration, document verification, choice filling, and seat allotment phases. The Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG), Rajasthan, will release the official notification and timeline on their official portal.

Key Phases of REAP Counselling

1. Online Registration

  • Candidates must complete their initial registration by paying the application fee of ₹295/- (tentative) before the deadline.
  • Fill in all personal, academic, and contact details accurately.

2. Choice Filling & Locking

  • After registration, candidates need to fill their preferred colleges and branches.
  • It is highly recommended to fill maximum choices and lock them before the last date. If not locked manually, they will be auto-locked.

3. Merit List Declaration

  • A combined merit list based on JEE Main scores and Class 12th board marks will be published.
  • Candidates can raise grievances if there are discrepancies in their merit rank.
Important Instructions Regarding Deadlines

Missing a deadline can lead to disqualification from the counselling process. Keep the following points in mind:

  • Frequent Checking: The schedule is subject to change. Regularly check the official REAP website for updates and extensions.
  • Fee Payment: Ensure the application and counselling fees are paid well before the last date to avoid server issues.
  • Reporting: Physical reporting to the allotted college within the specified timeframe is mandatory to confirm admission. Failure to do so will result in seat cancellation.
