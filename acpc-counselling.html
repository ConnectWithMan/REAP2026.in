ACPC Counselling 2026

Gujarat Engineering Counselling - Admissions & Counselling

Centralized counselling for engineering and technical institutes across Gujarat

Overview

ACPC Counselling 2026 is the centralized admission and counselling process conducted by the Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC), Gujarat for engineering and technical institutes across Gujarat. The portal streamlines registration, choice filling, seat allotment and reporting to participating colleges. The process aims to allocate seats transparently based on merit, GUJCET scores, reservation rules and candidate preferences.

Eligibility Criteria for ACPC Counselling 2026
  • Eligibility varies by program and institute; check the official ACPC notification for exact criteria.
  • For B.Tech: 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics and Mathematics along with one of Chemistry/Biology/Computer Science. Valid GUJCET score required.
  • For B.Arch: 10+2 qualified with valid GUJCET-Architecture score.
  • Lateral entry: Diploma holders may be eligible for second-year admission as per institute rules.
  • Age limits, domicile and category-specific relaxations are applied as per the official ACPC guidelines and Gujarat norms.
How to Apply for ACPC Counselling 2026

Candidates wanting to participate in ACPC Counselling 2026 should follow the steps below to complete online registration:

  • Step 1: Registration: Register on the official ACPC portal using a valid email and mobile number.
  • Step 2: Fee Payment: Pay the required counselling registration fee online.
  • Step 3: Application Form: Fill in personal, academic, GUJCET details and category information accurately.
  • Step 4: Document Upload: Upload scanned copies of mark sheets, GUJCET score card, identity proof, caste certificate and photograph.
  • Step 5: Merit Ranking: Merit ranking will be prepared based on GUJCET scores and category.
  • Step 6: Choice Filling: Select preferred colleges and branches. Use the full choice matrix to maximize options.
  • Step 7: Choice Locking: Lock choices before the deadline to confirm preferences.
  • Step 8: Seat Allotment: Seats are allotted based on merit, GUJCET scores, reservation, and choice preferences.
  • Step 9: Reporting: Report to the allotted institute and complete document verification and fee payment.
Documents Required

Candidates must submit the following documents online and carry originals during institute reporting:

  • 10th Class Marksheet and Certificate
  • 12th Class Marksheet and Certificate
  • Diploma Marksheet and Certificate (if applicable)
  • GUJCET Score Card (Gujarat Common Entrance Test)
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, etc.)
  • Domicile Certificate of Gujarat (if applicable)
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD) issued by competent authority
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Signature

Reservation Policy

Reservation is provided as per Gujarat government rules:

  • SC (Scheduled Caste)
  • ST (Scheduled Tribe)
  • OBC (Other Backward Classes)
  • EWS (Economically Weaker Section)
  • PwD (Persons with Disability)
  • Sports Quota
  • Girls' quota and other local category rules as applicable
