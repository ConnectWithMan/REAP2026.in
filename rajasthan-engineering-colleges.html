Engineering Education in Rajasthan

Rajasthan has emerged as a significant destination for technical education in North India. The state is home to a vast network of Government and Private Engineering Institutes that offer undergraduate programs like B.E./B.Tech and B.Arch. The majority of these institutions are affiliated with either Rajasthan Technical University (RTU), Kota or Bikaner Technical University (BTU). Admissions are centralized and managed through the REAP Counselling Process, which utilizes JEE Main ranks and Class 12th board percentages. Educational hubs like Jaipur, Jodhpur, Kota, and Udaipur host some of the premier engineering campuses.

Eligibility Criteria for REAP 2026

To participate in the Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process, candidates must meet specific academic requirements set by the technical board.

For B.Tech / B.E. Admissions

  • Qualifying Exam: Candidates must have passed the Class 12th (Intermediate) examination or equivalent.
  • Compulsory Subjects: Physics and Mathematics are mandatory. One optional subject from Chemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, or Technical Vocational subjects is required.
  • Minimum Marks: A minimum aggregate of 45% marks is required (40% for candidates belonging to SC/ST/Non-Creamy Layer OBC/Non-Creamy Layer MBC of Rajasthan State).

For B.Arch Admissions

  • Qualifying Exam: 10+2 scheme of examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics or 10+3 Diploma Examination with Mathematics as a compulsory subject.
  • Minimum Marks: At least 50% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics and also at least 50% marks in aggregate of the 10+2 examination.
  • Aptitude Test: A valid score in NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture) is mandatory.
Application Procedure for REAP 2026

The application process for REAP is entirely online. Candidates seeking admission to engineering colleges in Rajasthan should follow this step-by-step guide:

  • Registration: Navigate to the official REAP web portal. Register using active contact details (Email and Mobile Number).
  • Payment of Fees: Process the application fee payment of Rs. 590/- (approx). Modes accepted typically include Net Banking, Credit/Debit Cards, or E-Mitra Kiosks.
  • Form Filling: Access the application form using the challan number. Enter personal details, academic scores (JEE Main/12th), and category information carefully.
  • Document Uploading: Scan and upload necessary documents like photographs, signatures, and mark sheets ensuring they meet the specified file size and format.
  • Choice Filling: This is crucial. Select your preferred colleges and branches. Arrange them in descending order of preference.
  • Final Locking: Review the choices and lock them before the last date. Unlocked choices are usually auto-locked by the system on the deadline.
Documents Required

Candidates must produce the following original documents along with self-attested copies at the time of reporting to the allotted institute:

  • Aadhaar Card or valid Photo ID proof.
  • 10th Class Marksheet and Certificate (Date of Birth Proof).
  • 12th Class Marksheet and Certificate.
  • JEE Main 2026 Score Card (if admission via JEE Main).
  • Domicile Certificate (for Rajasthan candidates).
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS) issued by competent authority.
  • Income Certificate (for TFWS candidates).
  • Medical Fitness Certificate.
Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Which is the best government college?

MBM University, Jodhpur and RTU Kota are widely considered the best government engineering colleges in Rajasthan.

Q: What is the fee in private colleges?

The average fee varies from 70,000 to 1.5 Lakhs INR per annum depending on the college and facilities.

Q: Is there a separate exam for Rajasthan colleges?

No, admissions are based on JEE Main Rank or Class 12th Percentage via the REAP counselling process.

Q: Which city is best for engineering in Rajasthan?

Jaipur is the hub with the maximum number of top-rated private colleges and good industry connectivity.
